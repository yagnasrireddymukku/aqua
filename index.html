Common Questions

How often do I need to change filters?

We recommend changing the sediment and carbon filters every 6 months, and the RO membrane every 12-18 months depending on usage.

Does it require electricity?

Yes, the AquaMotion system uses a low-voltage pump to push water through the RO membrane and power the UV purification.

Is installation included?

Yes! Professional installation is included with all Home Pro and Enterprise models within our service areas.
